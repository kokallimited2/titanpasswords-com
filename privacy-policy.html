Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect ZERO personal data. Our password generator runs entirely in your browser (client-side). No passwords, no form data, no personal information is sent to our servers.

We use Cloudflare for CDN and DNS services — Cloudflare may collect anonymised analytics data (page views, approximate geographic region, browser type). This data is not personally identifiable.

Cookies

This website does NOT set first-party cookies. Cloudflare may set essential cookies for security (bot detection, DDoS protection). These are strictly functional and do not track you.

Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare for content delivery. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to their processing of connection data.

Our affiliate links (1Password, Dashlane, Bitwarden, LastPass) use their respective affiliate platforms — those platforms may set cookies for commission attribution.

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or port. If you have concerns about Cloudflare's data processing, contact Cloudflare directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
